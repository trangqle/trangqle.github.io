Nhai Ben Magazine

Client: FPT High School

Year: 2016-2020

Nhai Ben Magazine is a magazine run by the students of FPT High School (Hanoi, Vietnam). Inspired by magazines like T-pot Journal and Kinfolk, Nhai Ben offers a look into the life of FPT students, along with various articles about lifestyles, education, etc.
I worked on this magazine as the lead designer from 2016 to 2019, and from 2019 onwards I work alongside other members as a mentor.
