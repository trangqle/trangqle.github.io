Born in Hanoi, Vietnam, based in Winnipeg, I’m a third year student in Studio Art, BFA program at School of Art, University of Manitoba, with concentration in Graphic Design. Outside of school I’m a freelance designer — been running for 4 years now and loving every moment of it!

To me, design is as much about functionality and efficiency as it is about beauty. To achieve that delicate balance is not an easy task, but it is what I aim to deliver in all my works.

Besides design, I love dogs and coffee. Especially coffee.
